DIAGNOSIS — NOT a missing-sample bug
------------------------------------
Canonicalization (verified empirically) collapses both GM 22 and GM 42 to render
note 42 (hihat_closed), and both GM 26 and GM 46 to render note 46 (hihat_open).
Yet on DRSKit, render-42 sounds at peak 0.327 in one slot and is dead silent in
another 3.5 s later — the SAME render note, same render, different outcome. The
sample plainly exists. The phantoms are therefore a time/sequence-dependent
DrumGizmo hi-hat behaviour (choke group / voice-stealing across closed/open/pedal),
NOT a kit lacking the articulation. All 4 phantoms are hi-hat; every other bus on
every kit is OK. Sfizz (no choke modelling) is fully clean.

Secondary observation: many hi-hat_open / hi-hat_pedal hits clear the 1e-4 gate but
are near-silent (peak 0.002-0.005) on the DG kits — the threshold is lenient for
the hi-hat family specifically.
